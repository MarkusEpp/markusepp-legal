3. What Data is Collected?

3.1 Local Storage (on your device)

The app stores the following data only locally on your device:

  • Game settings: Start time, player names (that you enter), category settings
  • Game scores: Points during a game session

This data is stored using AsyncStorage (local storage) and never leaves your device. It is not transmitted to us or any third parties.

3.2 No Personal Data

We do NOT collect:

  • Names, email addresses, or contact information
  • Location data
  • Photos or files from your device
  • Usage statistics or analytics
  • Advertising IDs or tracking data

4. How is Data Used?

The locally stored data (player names, settings) is used exclusively to restore your game settings the next time you open the app. That's it!

5. Data Sharing with Third Parties

NO data is shared with third parties. The app contains no analytics tools, no advertising networks, and no external services that could collect data.

6. Data Security

Since all data is stored only locally on your device, security is in your hands. We recommend protecting your device with a passcode/Touch ID/Face ID.

7. Your Rights

Since we don't collect personal data, there's no central database you need to access. You can:

8. Children

The app is suitable for users of all ages and does not collect personal data. However, parents should monitor the player names their children enter.
